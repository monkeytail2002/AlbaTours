Isle of Rum

See the islands of Rum, Eigg and Muck via our ferry. There are multiple activities that can be done on the islands that will be fun for all the family. Go sight seeing for wildlife on the Rum nature reserve; or visit Muck for the hauntingly beautiful landscapes. Climb an Sgurr in Eigg, the largest pitchstone ride in Europe! There's plenty to see or do on the islands.
